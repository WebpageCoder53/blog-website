PLACES MUST VISIT

Prithvi Cafe

I have not visited this place in 2018.

Quaint little restaurant tucked inside a by-lane of Juhu. Blink and you will miss the entrance to the cafe. So be alert enough to spot the black signboard that says "Prithvi cafe" while you are on the main road. The restaurant has a reasonably good menu at reasonable prices. The food is delicious, the servings are quite large and some dishes can be shared by 2 people. Attracts quite a trendy crowd of aspiring celebrities on show days. Be sure to book in advance to avoid disappointments. On other days, the crowd there is pretty hep & happening. Hosts the famous "Prithvi theater fest", which you must attend if possible.


Jantar Mantar Jaipur

I have also not visited this place in 2019.

It is a wonderful place. Must visit this place. Hiring a tour guide will be better to understand the stories of such a historical place. It is a beautifully maintained small complex with yellow painted walls. It is a historical site and famous among tourists. It contains a number of different devices that were used to measure time in different eras in the Indian history. It has a nominal ticket price of ₹15 for Indians but about ₹225-250 for foreigners. Nice place to visit. We had an incredible experience visiting this observatory and seeing the accuracy of the astronomical instruments built around 300 years ago.

Review on the Movies

Avengers - Endgame
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride.

You must have a minimal previous understanding about the story, or you will miss 40% of the fun. You should watch Infinity war, civil war, and wasp before watching avengers - endgame. The entire movie pays homage to most of the other marvel superhero movies. you will enjoy it a lot. The movie is based oon the most talked action packed sci-fi series. The movie shows avengers-assemble Once more in order to reverse Thanos' action and restore balance to the universe. The movie is lengthy but its all worthwhile.

The Lion King
Nice for one-time watch

The kids would love this movie, The story shows how the young lion prince after the murder of his father, flees his kingdom only to learn the true meaning of responsibility and bravery. This movie is worth a watch for its visuals and cinematography.

Ice Age
This film is obviously an instant classic.

There's a group of 3 completely different animals trying to coexist together and bring a lost baby child back to his father after his mother passed away. I loved every scene of this movie, like when Sid, Manny, and Diego are fighting with the birds over the watermelon for their baby, and the scenes when Sid upsets the Rhinos and they literally try to murder him.
